About Kellie

I believe the therapeutic relationship is an important part of the process and I strive to create a safe and respectful environment for you to share your journey. My approach is collaborative, meaning we work together to identify goals and the best path forward for you.

I have extensive experience working with adolescents, adults, and families dealing with anxiety, depression, parenting struggles, and self-esteem issues. I draw from various evidence-based modalities, including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Psychodynamic approaches, to tailor treatment to your specific needs.
